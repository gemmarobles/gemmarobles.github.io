About me

I am a tenured professor (Profesora Contratada Doctora) in the Department of Psychology, Sociology and Philosophy at the University of León. I was previously a Ramón y Cajal researcher at the University of León, and before that I was a Juan de la Cierva researcher at the University of La Laguna. I received my PhD in Philosophy from the University of Salamanca.

My research work is focused on philosophical logic, especially on substructural logics, relevance logics, consistency and paraconsistency.
