About me

I am an Associate Professor (Profesora Titular de Universidad) in the Departamento de Psicología, Sociología y Filosofía at the Universidad de León (UNILEON or ULE). I was previously a Profesora Contratada Doctora, and before that, a Ramón y Cajal Research Fellow at the Universidad de León (UNILEON). And before that, I was a Juan de la Cierva Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of La Laguna (ULL). I received my PhD in Philosophy from the University of Salamanca (USAL).

My research work is focused on philosophical logic, especially on substructural logics, relevance logics, consistency and paraconsistency.

I am a member of the editorial board of Logique et Analyse.

My full name is Gemma Robles Vázquez (Robles is my first surname and Vázquez is my second surname), but professionally, I normally use only my first surname, for pragmatical reasons.
